Integrals

Hidden in a nook of the skull lies an artifact and two slots in the skull, both of which seem like perfect fits for the cup. you deduce that the skull wants you to make a replica of the artifact using a near by stash of clay. Although the shape of the cup is unusual, it can be replicated by the graphs $$f(x)=-\frac{1}{10}\left(x+2.4\right)^{2}+6.6$$ $$g(x) =\frac{1}{10}\left(6-x\right)^{2}+1$$ when 0 < x < 10, these graphs can be seen to the right. Note that because we just need outer contour for the replica, the cup does not need to be hollow.
